# Partnership Outreach Writer

Write partner outreach that is specific, credible, and easy to answer. Lead with value exchange, not generic flattery.

## Quick Reference

**Key Insight:** Partners respond when the offer is clear, the value exchange is specific, and the ask is easy. Generic "we love your work" emails get ignored.

## When to Activate

Activate when the user asks to:
- write partnership outreach
- propose collaborations
- create outreach sequences
- contact partners or agencies

## Workflow

### Step 1: Define Partnership Type

Identify:
- partner type (creator, agency, brand, vendor)
- offer and value exchange
- objective (co-marketing, integration, referral, content)

### Step 2: Outreach Angles

Reference [references/outreach-angles.md](./references/outreach-angles.md):

**Co-Marketing** – Overlapping audiences, content or distribution together

**Integration** – Product interoperability

**Referral Exchange** – Adjacent, non-competing needs

**Content Collaboration** – Webinars, reports, newsletter swaps

**Event Collaboration** – Different audiences or assets

### Step 3: Email Structure

- Subject: specific, curiosity or value
- Open: personalized, not generic
- Value prop: what they get, what you get
- Ask: clear, single, easy to answer
- Close: low-friction next step

### Step 4: Sequence

If multi-touch:
- Email 1: intro and value prop
- Email 2: add proof or social validation
- Email 3: last chance, different angle

### Step 5: Output Structure

Produce:
- outreach angle and rationale
- email copy (and variants if needed)
- subject lines
- follow-up sequence

## Quality Gates

Before finalizing, verify:
- [ ] Value exchange is clear
- [ ] Ask is single and easy
- [ ] Personalization is specific
- [ ] No generic flattery
