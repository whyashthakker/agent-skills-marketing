# Brand Voice Example

Input:
"Define a voice guide for a modern B2B marketing platform serving agencies and
in-house teams."

Expected output shape:

- 3 to 4 voice pillars
- a list of phrases or habits to avoid
- example rewrites for homepage and email copy
- notes on how the voice shifts across channels

Good answer qualities:

- the voice is specific enough to change writing behavior
- examples demonstrate the rules clearly
