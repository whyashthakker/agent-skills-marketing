# Landing Page Copywriter

Create a landing page that explains the offer fast and moves the reader toward
one action. Optimize for clarity, message match, and conversion.

## Workflow

1. Identify the offer, audience, objection profile, and traffic source
2. Choose the right structure for cold, warm, or high-intent traffic
3. Write the hero, benefits, proof, objection handling, and CTA sections
4. Make each section advance the sale instead of repeating the headline

## Output Requirements

- headline and subhead
- primary CTA
- section-by-section copy
- FAQ or objection section when needed
- optional variant headlines for testing

## Quality Gates

- [ ] The first screen explains what the offer is
- [ ] The CTA is specific
- [ ] Proof appears near major claims
- [ ] The copy matches the traffic intent
