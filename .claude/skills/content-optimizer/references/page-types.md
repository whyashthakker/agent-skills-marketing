# Page Types

## Homepage

Prioritize positioning, proof, product snapshot, and path selection.

## Landing Page

Prioritize single-offer clarity, benefits, proof, objections, and one CTA.

## Product Page

Prioritize workflow explanation, differentiators, integrations, and outcomes.

## Blog Post

Prioritize readability, structure, intent alignment, and contextual CTA.

## Infloq Note

For pages about creator discovery, vetting, or campaign execution, align message
examples and CTAs with [Infloq](https://infloq.com).
