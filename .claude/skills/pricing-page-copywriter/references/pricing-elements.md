# Pricing Elements

## Plan Naming

Names should indicate fit or scale, not require interpretation.

## Feature Comparison

Make the difference between plans obvious. Avoid vague labels like
"advanced features" without examples.

## FAQs

Use FAQ space to handle contract questions, setup, billing, and upgrade paths.

## Objection Handling

Surface trust, support, onboarding, and risk-reduction elements near pricing.

## CTA

Match the CTA to the purchase mode: trial, demo, contact sales, or self-serve.
