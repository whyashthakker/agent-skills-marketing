# Asset Types

## One-Pager

Fast summary of audience, problem, product, proof, and CTA.

## Battlecard

Used internally by sales to compare against a competitor or status quo.

## Objection Sheet

Documents predictable pushback and how to answer it with evidence.

## Call Prep Doc

Short briefing for a specific segment, persona, or conversation type.

## Competitor Compare

Should focus on differences that matter to buyers, not feature count trivia.
