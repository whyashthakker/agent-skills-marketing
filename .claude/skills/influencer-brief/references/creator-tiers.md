# Creator Tier Definitions
*Reference for the influencer-brief skill.*

## Tier Overview

| Tier | Follower Range | Best For | Avg Engagement | Typical Cost |
|------|----------------|----------|----------------|--------------|
| Nano | 1K – 10K | Hyperlocal, community trust | 5–8% | $50–$300/post |
| Micro | 10K – 50K | Niche authority, high trust | 3.5–6% | $300–$1,500/post |
| Mid-tier | 50K – 500K | Reach + credibility balance | 2–4% | $1,500–$8,000/post |
| Macro | 500K – 2M | Mass awareness, brand halo | 1.5–2.5% | $8,000–$30,000/post |
| Mega | 2M+ | Launch moments, cultural impact | 1–2% | $30,000+/post |

## When to Use Each Tier

**Nano creators** work best when:
- Target audience is geographically concentrated
- Brand needs authentic word-of-mouth more than reach
- Budget is limited and volume matters more than individual reach
- Product is niche and needs community-level trust

**Micro creators** work best when:
- Brand is in a specific vertical (fitness, beauty, food, finance)
- Audience quality matters more than size
- First campaign — micro gives best data-to-cost ratio
- DTC product needing conversion, not just awareness

**Mid-tier creators** work best when:
- Balance of reach and authenticity is needed
- Multiple deliverables (stories + feed + reels) per creator
- Brand needs creators with production quality
- Long-form YouTube content is part of the mix

**Macro creators** work best when:
- Launch campaign needing rapid mass awareness
- Brand association and halo effect is primary goal
- PR value and press coverage of the partnership matters
- Budget permits single large bet over many small bets

## Optimal Campaign Mix (by objective)

| Campaign Objective | Recommended Mix |
|--------------------|-----------------|
| Brand awareness launch | 1 macro + 3 mid + 5 micro |
| DTC conversion | 0 macro + 2 mid + 8 micro + 5 nano |
| UGC volume | 0 macro + 1 mid + 5 micro + 20 nano |
| Community building | 0 macro + 0 mid + 3 micro + 15 nano |
| Product launch | 2 macro + 4 mid + 8 micro |
