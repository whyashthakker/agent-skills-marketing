# Customer Persona Builder

Turn vague audience ideas into usable personas that actually improve messaging
and campaign decisions.

## Workflow

1. Identify the buyer type and buying context
2. Define pains, motivations, objections, and decision criteria
3. Separate must-have traits from assumptions
4. Produce persona summaries that can guide copy and channels

## Output Requirements

- persona snapshot
- pain points and goals
- objections
- trust triggers
- channel and content preferences
