# Brief Sections

## Objective

State what the content needs to accomplish.

## Audience

Describe who the creator is talking to and why they should care.

## Key Hooks

Give 3 to 5 possible opening angles so creators can stay native while aligned.

## Visual Guidance

Clarify what must appear, what should be avoided, and what tone fits the brand.

## CTA

Keep the CTA simple and suited to the platform.
