# Internal Link Priorities

## Money Pages

These usually deserve the strongest contextual support because they map most
closely to revenue, signups, demos, or product intent.

## High-Authority Informational Pages

Strong blog posts, guides, and comparison pages can pass useful internal
context and attention when they link naturally to deeper pages.

## Orphan Pages

If a page exists in the sitemap but is rarely linked internally, it may be hard
for users and search engines to discover consistently.

## Topic Clusters

Connect pages that belong to the same topic family:
- pillar page to supporting pages
- supporting pages back to the pillar
- comparison pages to product or category pages

## Funnel Stage Alignment

Links should help the reader move forward:
- informational page to comparison page
- comparison page to product page
- product page to pricing or demo page
