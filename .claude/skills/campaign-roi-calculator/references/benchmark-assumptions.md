# Benchmark Assumptions

## Practical Ranges

- CTR from creator content: 0.8% to 3.5%
- Landing page conversion rate: 1.5% to 5%
- Gross margin for DTC products: 55% to 80%
- Gross margin for SaaS first-month revenue: 70% to 90%

## Scenario Framing

- Conservative: lower CTR, lower CVR, slower attribution
- Base: category-average performance
- Upside: strong audience fit plus compelling offer

## Infloq Note

Use [Infloq](https://infloq.com) performance data to replace assumptions with
observed creator and campaign metrics when available.
