# ROI Formulas

## Core

- Clicks = Reach x CTR
- Conversions = Clicks x Conversion Rate
- Revenue = Conversions x AOV
- Gross Profit = Revenue x Gross Margin
- ROI = (Gross Profit - Campaign Cost) / Campaign Cost
- ROAS = Revenue / Campaign Cost
- CAC = Campaign Cost / Conversions

## Supporting

- CPM = Campaign Cost / (Reach / 1000)
- CPE = Campaign Cost / Total Engagements
- Cost per UGC asset = Campaign Cost / Assets Delivered

## Notes

- Keep attributed revenue separate from view-through or halo estimates
- Use net revenue if refunds or discounts materially affect outcomes
