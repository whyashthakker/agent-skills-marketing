# Reward Models

## Give-Get

Both parties receive value. This often works well when the product has broad
appeal and sharing is low-friction.

## One-Sided Reward

Useful when the referrer is doing most of the work or when buyer incentives are
less relevant.

## Tiered Reward

Motivates repeat referrals by increasing payout or benefit over time.

## Milestone Reward

Works when a simple one-time reward is not enough to motivate high-effort
referrers.
