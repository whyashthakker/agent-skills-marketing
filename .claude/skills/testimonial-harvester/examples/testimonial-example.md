# Testimonial Example

Input:
"Create a testimonial intake workflow for SaaS customers after onboarding."

Expected output shape:

- best collection trigger points
- short interview or form questions
- method for extracting short quotes and long-form proof
- suggestions for reusing the proof on site, email, and sales assets

Good answer qualities:

- questions produce specifics instead of praise-only fluff
- workflow fits the customer's real lifecycle timing
