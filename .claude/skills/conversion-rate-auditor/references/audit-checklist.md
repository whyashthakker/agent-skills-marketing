# Audit Checklist

## Value Clarity

Can a new visitor understand what the offer is within a few seconds?

## CTA Clarity

Is the next step obvious and specific, or does the page rely on vague verbs?

## Proof Placement

Are testimonials, logos, data points, or case proof located near the claims
that need support?

## Objection Handling

Does the page answer obvious concerns such as pricing, setup effort, trust, or
fit?

## Form Friction

Are there unnecessary fields, unclear expectations, or too much commitment too
early?
