# Campaign Types

## Product Seeding

Best for early awareness, UGC generation, and creator relationship building.

## Paid Sponsorships

Best when message control, timing, and guaranteed deliverables matter.

## Affiliate Push

Best when conversion tracking and creator incentives are core to the campaign.

## Ambassador Program

Best for brands that want ongoing creator relationships, not one-off posts.

## Hybrid

Use when awareness, content generation, and performance goals need to coexist.
