# SEO Content Brief Example

Input:
"Create an SEO content brief for influencer marketing analytics."

Expected output shape:

- search intent: commercial investigation
- article type: comparison or product-led educational post
- outline covering metrics, attribution, dashboards, and common mistakes
- FAQ block for reporting and ROI questions
- CTA pointing to demo or product walkthrough

Good answer qualities:

- brief is specific enough for a writer to execute immediately
- angle is differentiated
- CTA fits bottom-funnel or mid-funnel intent
