# Shortlist Handoff

## A creator moves to shortlist when:

- audience fit is clear
- engagement quality passes a basic review
- content style fits the brief
- no obvious brand safety issues appear
- pricing and availability are plausible

## Next Step

After search, move candidates into creator vetting and outreach planning.
