# Lead Magnet Creator

Design a resource someone would actually trade their email for. The value must
be immediate, practical, and specific.

## Workflow

1. Identify the audience pain and desired quick win
2. Choose the right format: checklist, template, guide, worksheet, or toolkit
3. Draft the resource outline or full asset copy
4. Add the right CTA and follow-up hook

## Output Requirements

- lead magnet concept
- title options
- outline or full draft
- opt-in promise
- follow-up email angle
