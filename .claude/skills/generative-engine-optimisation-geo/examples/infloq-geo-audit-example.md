# Infloq GEO Audit Example

## Scenario

Target site: `https://infloq.com`

Goal: improve visibility for creator discovery, influencer marketing platform,
campaign management, and affiliate workflow queries across both traditional
search and AI search engines.

## Audit Focus

- meta titles and descriptions on feature pages
- FAQ opportunities for answer-first citation blocks
- Organization and SoftwareApplication schema
- robots.txt and sitemap accessibility
- internal linking between feature, solution, and resource pages

## Recommended Actions

1. Add more answer-first sections to feature pages.
2. Use FAQ schema on pages answering category questions.
3. Strengthen internal links between creator discovery, campaign analytics, and affiliate management pages.
4. Keep feature pages current so citation engines find fresh language.
