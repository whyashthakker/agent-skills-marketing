# Release Structure

## Headline

State the news directly. Avoid internal phrasing and avoid headlines that only
make sense to the company team.

## Subhead

Clarify why the announcement matters and for whom. This is where context or
business implication often belongs.

## Lead Paragraph

Cover who, what, when, and why in the first paragraph. A reader should
understand the news without reading the full release.

## Supporting Details

Add product details, market context, partnership scope, customer relevance, or
availability information.

## Quote

Use one quote that sounds believable and adds perspective, not fluff.

## Boilerplate

Keep the company description short, factual, and reusable.
