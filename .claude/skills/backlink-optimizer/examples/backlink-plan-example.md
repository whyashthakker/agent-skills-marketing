# Backlink Plan Example

Input:
"Build a backlink strategy for a creator analytics platform targeting agencies
and e-commerce brands."

Expected output shape:

- 3 priority pages worth building links to
- 2 linkable asset concepts such as a benchmark report or template pack
- target categories: creator-economy blogs, agency resources, SaaS review sites
- 30-day action plan and 90-day authority-building roadmap

Good answer qualities:

- links are tied to pages with business value
- target sites are relevant, not generic high-DA lists
- the asset ideas are specific enough to execute
