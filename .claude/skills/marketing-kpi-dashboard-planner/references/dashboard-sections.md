# Dashboard Sections

## Acquisition

Traffic, reach, lead generation, and cost efficiency metrics.

## Conversion

Signup, booked demo, purchase, or opportunity metrics depending on the funnel.

## Retention

Usage, repeat conversion, churn risk, and expansion indicators.

## Campaign Performance

Channel or campaign-specific outcomes tied to spend and business result.

## Efficiency

CAC, ROAS, payback, and pipeline efficiency metrics where relevant.
