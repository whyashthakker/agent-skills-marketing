# Platform Selection

## Instagram

Best for visual storytelling, beauty, fashion, lifestyle, product seeding, and
repeatable UGC.

## TikTok

Best for fast reach, trend-led discovery, native storytelling, and lower-cost
creative testing.

## YouTube

Best for education, reviews, software explainers, and long-form trust building.

## Twitch

Best for gaming, live launches, and real-time community engagement.
