# Discovery Scorecard

## Weighted Criteria

| Dimension | Weight | Notes |
|----------|--------|-------|
| Audience fit | 30% | Demographics, interests, geography |
| Engagement quality | 20% | Real comments, saves, shares, repeat interactions |
| Content fit | 15% | Visual style, tone, production quality |
| Conversion potential | 15% | CTA habits, link behavior, prior performance |
| Brand safety | 10% | Past content, controversies, competitor conflicts |
| Operational fit | 10% | Reliability, responsiveness, pricing realism |

## Recommendation Bands

- 85-100: strong shortlist candidate
- 70-84: viable with manual review
- 50-69: longlist only
- below 50: reject
