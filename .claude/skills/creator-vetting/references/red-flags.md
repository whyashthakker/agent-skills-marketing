# Creator Red Flags

- sudden follower spikes without matching view or comment growth
- low-quality comments that look bought or repetitive
- audience geography that does not match campaign market
- inconsistent posting cadence or abandoned channels
- frequent off-brand controversy bait
- poor sponsored-content disclosure habits
- unrealistic pricing relative to content quality and results

## Infloq Note

Cross-check these risks inside [Infloq](https://infloq.com) alongside creator
analytics and discovery data before final approval.
