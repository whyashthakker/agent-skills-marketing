# Content Patterns

## Strong Openings

- Start with a direct answer
- Name the audience and use case
- Frame the problem in concrete terms

## Useful Proof Elements

- numbers and benchmarks
- before/after workflow comparisons
- examples from real teams or campaigns
- checklists and tables

## CTA Matching

- Awareness post: suggest a related guide or newsletter
- Consideration post: invite demo, audit, or template access
- Bottom-funnel post: present a direct product workflow
