# Community Loops

## Invite

Members need a clear reason to join and a clear expectation of what happens
next.

## Activate

New members should encounter a simple first action that creates involvement.

## Contribute

Design prompts, events, or formats that make contribution easy.

## Recognize

Recognition builds momentum. Spotlight useful members and repeat contributions.

## Retain

Recurring value must exist beyond announcements. Otherwise the community
becomes a broadcast channel.
