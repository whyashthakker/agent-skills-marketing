# Funnel Stages

## Registration

Clarify the topic, audience, and expected takeaway. Registration pages convert
better when the promise is narrow and outcome-based.

## Reminder Sequence

Use 3 to 4 reminders spread across signup confirmation, 24 hours before,
1 hour before, and 10 minutes before. Keep each reminder distinct.

## Live Session

Open with the practical value, not company background. The CTA should connect
to the content, not interrupt it.

## Replay

Treat replay as a second conversion window. Include a time-bound reason to act.

## Follow-Up

Segment follow-up by attendee, no-show, and high-intent actions such as asking
a question or clicking the offer.
